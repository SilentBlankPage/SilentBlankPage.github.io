LogSolve 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

最后更新日期 / Last Updated: 2026-02-07

中文版本

概述

LogSolve（以下简称“本应用”）尊重并保护用户隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何处理信息。

离线运行与网络访问

  • 本应用默认离线运行。
  • 仅在获取订阅商品信息、验证/恢复购买时与 Apple App Store 通信。
  • 我们不自建服务器，不会把日志或配置上传到我们的服务器。

我们处理的信息

我们不会收集或存储您的个人身份信息。

本应用不会：

  • 使用第三方分析或追踪工具
  • 展示广告或植入追踪代码
  • 访问通讯录、相册、位置等敏感权限

订阅与购买信息

  • 订阅/购买由 Apple App Store 处理。
  • 我们不会获取您的支付信息。
  • 应用仅能读取购买状态（是否为 Pro 及所属类型），用于解锁功能。

日志与配置数据的存储

  • 日志文件仅在设备本地处理，不会上传或共享。
  • 关键词、知识库、隐藏规则、时间格式、设置等数据保存在应用沙盒内（Apple 原生存储方案）。
  • 您可通过导出功能自行备份；删除应用会移除本地数据。

数据安全

  • 所有数据存储在本地沙盒中。
  • 我们不会运营任何服务器存储用户数据。
  • 数据安全由系统机制保障，但仍建议定期导出备份重要配置。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会主动收集儿童个人信息。

隐私政策变更

我们可能会更新本隐私政策。任何变更将在应用内提示，并更新“最后更新日期”。

联系我们

如有疑问，请联系：

邮箱：study_doc@foxmail.com

English Version

Overview

LogSolve (the “App”) respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information.

Offline Use & Network Access

  • The App runs fully offline by default.
  • It connects to Apple App Store only when fetching products or verifying/restoring purchases.
  • We do not run our own servers and do not upload logs or configurations.

Information We Handle

We do not collect or store your personal information.

The App does not:

  • Use third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • Display ads or embed tracking code
  • Access contacts, photos, location, or other sensitive permissions

Subscription & Purchase Information

  • Purchases are processed by Apple App Store.
  • We do not receive your payment details.
  • The App can only read purchase status (Pro and type) to unlock features.

Log & Configuration Storage

  • Log files are processed locally and never uploaded or shared.
  • Keywords, knowledge base, hide rules, time formats, and settings are stored in the app sandbox (Apple-native storage).
  • You can export data for backup; deleting the App removes local data.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally in the app sandbox.
  • We do not operate servers to store user data.
  • System protections apply, but we still recommend exporting backups regularly.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information.

Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be notified in the App and the “Last Updated” date will be revised.
