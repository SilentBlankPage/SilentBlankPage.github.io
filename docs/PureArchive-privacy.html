隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期（Effective Date）：2026-03-29

1. 概述 / Overview

我们重视你的隐私。Pure Archive 主要以本地文档方式运行，用于管理 App Store 元数据、数字资产、截图导出与相关工作流。

We value your privacy. Pure Archive operates primarily as a local document-based app for managing App Store metadata, digital assets, screenshot export workflows, and related tasks.

2. 我们处理的信息 / Information We Process

我们不会主动收集你的个人信息，也不会将你在应用中创建的内容默认上传到我们的服务器。

We do not actively collect your personal information, and we do not upload the content you create in the app to our servers by default.

  • 你创建或导入的 `.parch` 文档、App 元数据、本地化内容、数字资产、导出配置与备份文件，通常保存在你的设备或你主动选择的位置。
  • Your `.parch` documents, app metadata, localization content, digital assets, export settings, and backup files are generally stored on your device or in locations you explicitly choose.
  • 会员状态、部分本地偏好设置，以及用于本地 helper 协同的必要状态，可能保存在设备本地或 App Group 容器中。
  • Membership state, certain local preferences, and minimal state required for local helper coordination may be stored locally on device or in the App Group container.

3. 文件访问与本地存储 / File Access and Local Storage

Pure Archive 是文档型应用，会在你主动打开、保存、导入、导出或绑定目录时访问相关文件与文件夹。

Pure Archive is a document-based app and accesses files or folders when you explicitly open, save, import, export, or bind a directory.

  • 应用使用 macOS 沙盒与用户授权的读写权限访问你选择的文件位置。
  • The app uses the macOS sandbox and user-granted read/write access to reach file locations you choose.
  • 为方便后续继续访问你已授权的位置，应用可能保存安全作用域书签（security-scoped bookmarks）。
  • To continue accessing previously approved locations, the app may store security-scoped bookmarks.

4. 联网与可选服务 / Network Access and Optional Services

本应用本身不内置广告、统计或行为跟踪 SDK。联网通常只发生在以下场景：

The app does not embed ad, analytics, or behavioral tracking SDKs. Network activity typically occurs only in the following cases:

  • 你主动查看、购买或恢复会员服务时，由 Apple 的 App Store / StoreKit 处理相关网络请求。
  • When you view, purchase, or restore membership plans, the related network requests are handled by Apple's App Store / StoreKit services.
  • 你主动打开外部链接时，例如支持页面、隐私政策、服务条款或 App Store 页面。
  • When you intentionally open external links such as support pages, privacy policies, terms, or App Store pages.
  • 你主动配置并连接外部 AI / MCP 客户端或第三方服务时，相关数据是否会被发送给第三方，取决于你的配置和所使用服务的政策。
  • When you intentionally configure and connect external AI / MCP clients or third-party services, whether data is sent to those parties depends on your configuration and the policies of the services you use.

5. 第三方服务 / Third-Party Services

我们不接入第三方广告 SDK、统计 SDK 或跨应用行为跟踪服务。若你使用会员购买，相关交易由 Apple 处理；若你主动接入第三方 AI / MCP 工具，则相应数据处理受这些第三方的条款与隐私政策约束。

We do not integrate third-party ad SDKs, analytics SDKs, or cross-app behavioral tracking services. If you use membership purchases, the transactions are handled by Apple. If you intentionally connect third-party AI / MCP tools, related data processing is governed by those third parties' terms and privacy policies.

6. 你的控制权 / Your Controls

  • 你可以自行管理、导出、备份、移动或删除本地 `.parch` 文档与相关资源。
  • You may manage, export, back up, move, or delete your local `.parch` documents and related resources at any time.
  • 你可以停止授权目录访问，或删除你已导出的文件与备份。
  • You may stop granting directory access or delete files and backups you exported.
  • 你可以自行决定是否启用会员购买、恢复购买，或配置外部 AI / MCP 集成。
  • You may decide whether to enable membership purchases, restore purchases, or configure external AI / MCP integrations.

7. 政策更新 / Policy Updates

若本政策有更新，我们会在本页面更新最新版本与生效日期。

If this policy changes, we will update the latest version and effective date on this page.
