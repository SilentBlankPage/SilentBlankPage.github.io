# Privacy Policy

**App Name: ShotComposer**  
**Updated: 2026-02-21**

ShotComposer is an AppStore screenshot creation tool. We take your privacy seriously:

### 1. Data Collection and Storage
- **Local Processing**: All images processed by ShotComposer are generated locally on your device. We do not upload your images to any servers.

### 2. Permissions
- **Photo/Folder Access**: The app requires permission to read raw images and write exported screenshots.
