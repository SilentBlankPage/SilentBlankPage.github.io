## 中文版本

### 概述

LogSolve（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护用户的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们如何处理您的信息。

### 数据收集

**我们不收集任何个人数据。**

本应用：
- 不收集、存储或传输任何个人身份信息
- 不使用任何第三方分析或追踪工具
- 不包含任何广告或追踪代码
- 不访问您的联系人、照片、位置或其他敏感信息

### 日志文件处理

- 您打开的日志文件**仅在您的设备本地处理**
- 日志文件内容不会被上传到任何服务器
- 日志文件不会被分享给任何第三方
- 您可以随时删除应用及其所有数据

### iCloud 同步

本应用使用 Apple 的 iCloud 服务来同步您的配置数据（关键词、知识库、设置等）：
- iCloud 同步使用您的**私有 iCloud 数据库**
- 数据仅在您的设备之间同步
- 我们无法访问您的 iCloud 数据
- 您可以在 iOS 设置中关闭 iCloud 同步

### 订阅信息

如果您购买 Pro 订阅：
- 订阅由 Apple 的 App Store 处理
- 我们不收集或存储您的支付信息
- 订阅管理由 Apple 完全控制
- 我们只能访问订阅状态（是否为 Pro 用户），不能访问任何个人信息

### 数据安全

- 所有数据都存储在您的设备本地或您的私有 iCloud 账户中
- 我们不运营任何服务器来存储用户数据
- 您的数据安全由 iOS 和 iCloud 的安全机制保护

### 儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下的儿童，也不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

### 隐私政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更将在应用中通知，并更新"最后更新日期"。

### 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：
- 邮箱：[您的邮箱地址]

## English Version

### Overview

LogSolve (the "App") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

### Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App:
- Does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information
- Does not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools
- Does not contain any advertising or tracking code
- Does not access your contacts, photos, location, or other sensitive information

### Log File Processing

- Log files you open are **processed locally on your device only**
- Log file contents are not uploaded to any server
- Log files are not shared with any third parties
- You can delete the app and all its data at any time

### iCloud Sync

The App uses Apple's iCloud service to sync your configuration data (keywords, knowledge base, settings, etc.):
- iCloud sync uses your **private iCloud database**
- Data is synced only between your devices
- We cannot access your iCloud data
- You can disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings

### Subscription Information

If you purchase a Pro subscription:
- Subscriptions are processed by Apple's App Store
- We do not collect or store your payment information
- Subscription management is entirely controlled by Apple
- We can only access subscription status (whether you are a Pro user), not any personal information

### Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud account
- We do not operate any servers to store user data
- Your data security is protected by iOS and iCloud security mechanisms

### Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be notified in the app and the "Last Updated" date will be updated.
