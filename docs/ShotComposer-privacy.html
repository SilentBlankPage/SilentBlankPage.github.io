隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期（Effective Date）：2026-02-17

1. 我们是谁 / Who We Are

ShotComposer 是一款用于管理与编辑应用商店截图素材的桌面应用。

ShotComposer is a desktop app for managing and editing App Store screenshot assets.

2. 我们收集的信息 / Information We Collect

默认情况下，我们不要求你注册账号，也不主动收集你在设备本地处理的截图内容。在你主动联系我们时，我们会收到你提供的信息（例如邮箱地址和问题描述）。

By default, we do not require account registration and do not actively collect your locally processed screenshot content. If you contact us, we may receive information you provide, such as your email address and support message.

3. 信息用途 / How We Use Information

我们仅将你主动提供的信息用于以下用途：

  1. 回应你的支持请求 / Responding to support requests
  2. 排查与修复问题 / Troubleshooting and bug fixing
  3. 改进产品体验 / Improving product experience

4. 数据存储与安全 / Storage and Security

你的项目与图片数据默认保存在你的本地设备。我们会采取合理措施保护通过支持渠道收到的信息，防止未授权访问。

Your project and image data are stored on your local device by default. We take reasonable measures to protect information received through support channels from unauthorized access.

5. 第三方共享 / Third-Party Sharing

除非法律要求或为处理你的明确请求，我们不会出售或共享你的个人信息给第三方。

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or needed to fulfill your explicit request.

6. 数据保留与删除 / Data Retention and Deletion

我们仅在处理支持请求所需期间保留你主动提供的信息。你可随时通过邮件联系我们申请删除相关信息。

We retain information you provide only as long as needed to handle support requests. You may request deletion at any time via email.

7. 联系我们 / Contact

如需隐私相关帮助，请联系：study_doc@foxmail.com

For privacy-related requests, contact: study_doc@foxmail.com

8. 政策更新 / Policy Updates

我们可能会更新本政策。更新后会在本页面标注新的生效日期。

We may update this policy. When updated, the new effective date will be posted on this page.
